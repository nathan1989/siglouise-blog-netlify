We couldn't wait to share with our close family and friends the news of what gender Baby Sprout was.\
Inviting everyone to my parents house we shared an afternoon tea, everyone put their bet in for the gender they thought and my sister made a beautiful cake to celebrate.\
We did the reveal by letting balloons out of a box (which got stuck in a tree on the way up so we got to get them back down for photos - ha!) and then spent time afterwards sharing items of beautiful boys clothes we had purchased since finding out.

Thank you to everyone that came and shared this celebration with us.
